# Enqueue Job into Faktory Queue

## Primary actor

N/A

## Trigger

An upstream actor wants to insert a new job into the Faktory queue

## Goal

Insert a job into the Faktory job queue.

## Preconditions

The Faktory has been initialized.

## Scenario

1. An actor initiates a use case that creates a new job for distribution.
2. The job is inserted into the Faktory queue.
